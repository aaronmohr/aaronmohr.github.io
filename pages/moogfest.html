Moogfest

Festival Branding

Autumn 2016

Moogfest is an annual music festival that celebrates musicians and artist who work within more experimental means of electronic music/art. My project was to conceptually re-brand the festival and create a series of items that would accomodate the festival branding. My concept was the focus on a strictly digital aesthetic, working within a more free form space.
